Taipei Grand Mosque 台北清真寺
Taiwan's largest and most beautiful mosque — stunning Moorish domed architecture. Prayer facilities, wudu areas fully available. Peaceful and meaningful family visit. Great for photos with the boys. Non-Muslim visitors welcome with modest dress.

🚶 Walk from mosque ~15 min · or MRT Daan → CKS Memorial (1 stop)

Taiwan's most famous lake — extraordinary mountain scenery, crystal clear water, Thao indigenous culture. Pick up Sun Moon Lake Pass at the visitor center if not already purchased (includes cable car + lake bus). Main hub is Shuishe Pier.
11:30
Sun Moon Lake Ropeway (Cable Car) 🚡
Head to the gondola station first (10-min walk from Shuishe or short lakeside bus). 8-minute gondola ride with breathtaking panoramic views of the lake and surrounding mountains. Kids absolutely love it. Lines build after noon — going early is key.
💡 Opens 10:30am weekdays, 10am weekends. NT$300 round trip — included in Sun Moon Lake Pass. Return by gondola (don't continue to Aboriginal Village today — saves time for the boys).
13:00
Picnic lunch — Shuishe lakeside 🥪
Find a lakeside bench or grassy area at Shuishe for your packed halal lunch. Stunning views while eating. Boys can run on the waterfront promenade. This is genuinely one of the most beautiful lunch spots of the whole trip.
🟢 Your packed halal lunch — eat lakeside with mountain views
14:00

Hop-on hop-off lake boat from Shuishe Pier. First stop: Xuanguang Temple Pier — beautiful lakeside temple, great photos. Second stop: Ita Thao Pier (Thao indigenous village). NT$300 for all-day hop-on pass or NT$100/leg. Kids love the open-air boats on the water.
🚢 Lake Boat · all-day hop-on pass NT$300 · runs every 20–30 min
